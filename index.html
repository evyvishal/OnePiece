Portgas D. Ace

Ace, also known as Portgas D. Ace, is a beloved character in *One Piece*. He is the adopted older brother of Luffy and Sabo, and the son of the Pirate King, Gol D. Roger. Ace was the commander of Whitebeard’s 2nd division and possessed the Mera Mera no Mi, a Devil Fruit that allowed him to control fire. Brave, loyal, and strong-willed, Ace's journey was marked by his search for purpose and belonging. His tragic death during the Marineford War deeply impacted Luffy and remains one of the most emotional moments in the series.

Red-Haired Shanks

"Red-Haired" Shanks is one of the Four Emperors of the Sea and the captain of the Red-Haired Pirates in One Piece. His full name is Shanks, though his birth name remains unknown, and he is known for his calm demeanor, powerful Haki, and strong sense of honor.

Donquixote Doflamingo

Donquixote Doflamingo is a powerful and ruthless pirate in *One Piece*, known as the “Heavenly Yaksha.” He was a former Warlord of the Sea and ruled Dressrosa with manipulation and cruelty. Wielding the String-String Fruit, he could control people and even slice buildings with razor-sharp threads.
